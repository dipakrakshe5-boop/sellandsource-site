About Sell & Source

Our Mission

To revolutionize global B2B commerce by creating the most trusted, efficient, and comprehensive platform connecting Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) with qualified vendors and suppliers worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Sell & Source emerged from the recognition that traditional sourcing methods were inefficient, time-consuming, and often unreliable. Our founders, with decades of experience in manufacturing and supply chain management, envisioned a digital platform that would transform how businesses discover, evaluate, and partner with each other.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Rigorous Verification: Every company undergoes comprehensive background checks, certification reviews, and quality assessments before joining our platform.
  • AI-Powered Matching: Our proprietary algorithm analyzes requirements, capabilities, and compatibility to suggest the best potential partnerships.
  • Global Expertise: Local market knowledge combined with international reach, supporting businesses across 50+ countries.
  • End-to-End Support: From initial discovery to contract completion and ongoing relationship management.
  • Data Security: Enterprise-grade security ensuring all business information and communications remain confidential.

Our Values

Partnership: Success comes from long-term, mutually beneficial relationships.

Excellence: Commitment to the highest standards in everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sell & Source?

Sell & Source is a comprehensive B2B platform that connects Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) with verified vendors and suppliers globally. We facilitate secure partnerships through advanced matching technology, verification processes, and integrated communication tools.

How does the verification process work?

Our verification process includes business registration validation, financial background checks, quality certifications review, and reference verification. The process typically takes 3-5 business days and ensures all partners meet our quality standards.

What are the costs involved?

We offer a free starter plan with basic features. Our Professional plan at $99/month includes unlimited searches, advanced features, and priority support. Enterprise plans are customized based on specific needs.

Which industries do you serve?

We serve all major industries including Automotive, Electronics, Textiles, Machinery, Chemicals, Food & Beverages, Pharmaceuticals, and more. Our platform is designed to accommodate diverse manufacturing and sourcing needs.

How secure is my business information?

We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls. All data is protected under comprehensive privacy policies and international data protection standards. Your business information is never shared without explicit consent.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Yes! We offer API integrations and custom solutions for Enterprise clients. You can connect our platform with your ERP, CRM, or procurement systems for seamless workflow integration.
